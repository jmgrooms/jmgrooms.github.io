Top 2 Things To Do While Visiting Springfield, MO

1. Spend, at least, an entire afternoon wandering around Bass Pro Shops

If you didn't know, Bass Pro Shops was originally started in Springfield, MO. Located on the corner of East Sunshine St. and South Campbell Ave. it's the largest in the World. An entire afternoon might not be enough time to see it all, but it's a great start.

2. Get yourself a plate of cashew chicken

It's the unofficial dish of my beloved hometown, and an absolute cannot miss when you visit. Do your mouth a favor a go get some!

Why I Love Cashew Chicken

I'm originally from Springfield, MO, the birthplace of cashew chicken, and have eaten a plate of the delectable dish at least once a month for as far back as I can remember.

But, you don't have to have been born and raised in my hometown to appreciate those beautiful golden fried nuggets of chicken, coverd in a savory brown sauce, and topped with cashews and chopped green onions. All you have to do is visit Springfield, MO, the only place on Earth where you can find authentic cashew chicken, and get a plate of it yourself. One bite and you too shall sing it praise!
